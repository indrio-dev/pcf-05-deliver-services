## RESEARCH NOTES

### Shipping Regulations
- USDA regulates agricultural material from Hawaii to mainland - inspection required
- Some fruits must be treated at USDA-approved facilities
- Sharwil avocados from Hawaii can only ship to 33 mainland states
- Most farms use FedEx 2-Day or USPS Priority Mail
- Fresh fruit typically ships early week to avoid weekend delays

### Common Products NOT Found for Direct Mainland Shipping
- Fresh lychee (Kaye Family Farms ships Hawaii only due to regulations)
- Fresh mango (mostly through local Hawaii distributors)
- Many tropical fruits have shipping restrictions

### Price Points & Shipping
- Hawaii farms often offer free shipping thresholds: $75-150
- Puerto Rico benefits from domestic USPS rates
- Premium products: Coffee ($20-40/lb), Vanilla beans ($8-15/bean), Macadamia nuts ($15-30/lb)

### Best Product Categories for DTC
- Shelf-stable: Coffee, macadamia nuts, honey, vanilla, chocolate, sea salt
- Value-added: Jams, sauces, dried/processed fruits, flour
- Perishable but shippable: Fresh papaya, pineapple, goat cheese
- Flowers: Protea, lavender
