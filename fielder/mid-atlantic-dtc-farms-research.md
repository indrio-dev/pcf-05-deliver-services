## NOTES

1. **Shipping Seasonality**: Many farms adjust shipping schedules based on season and product perishability. Perishable items often require 2-day or overnight shipping during warm months.

2. **CSA Programs**: Most CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) programs run May-October with weekly pickup locations. Some offer delivery options for additional fees.

3. **Wine Shipping Regulations**:
   - **Pennsylvania**: Up to 36 cases/year per licensed direct wine shipper
   - **Virginia**: Up to 2 cases wine or beer/month from VA ABC licensed shippers
   - Both states require age 21+ signature and proper labeling

4. **Regional Specialties**:
   - Lancaster County: Amish/Mennonite foods dominate (scrapple, whoopie pies, shoofly pie, Lebanon bologna)
   - Chesapeake Bay: Blue crab and oysters are flagship products
   - Kennett Square: 60% of US mushroom production
   - Lake Erie: Largest grape growing region east of Rockies
   - Virginia Tidewater: Peanuts and Virginia ham are iconic
   - Adams County, PA: 6th largest apple/peach producer in nation

5. **Sustainability & Farming Practices**: Many farms emphasize:
   - Grass-fed/grass-finished (no grain)
   - Pasture-raised
   - Organic or chemical-free
   - No antibiotics, hormones, or growth additives
   - Sustainable/regenerative agriculture
   - Animal Welfare Approved certification

6. **Family Heritage**: Multi-generational farms are common:
   - 3rd-7th generation operations
   - Family recipes dating back 100-300 years
   - Traditional Amish/Mennonite farming methods

7. **Free Shipping Thresholds**: Common patterns:
   - Many offer free shipping on orders $75-$100+
   - Some offer flat-rate shipping ($9-$25)
   - Specialty shippers like Edwards VA Smokehouse offer free shipping on all orders

8. **Peak Seasons**:
   - Crabs: May-November (best season)
   - Oysters: Fall through spring
   - Apples/Peaches: August-October
   - Maple Syrup: Late winter/early spring production
   - Ramps (WV): Early spring only

9. **Unique Products Not Found Elsewhere**:
   - Lebanon Bologna (Lancaster/Lebanon County PA only)
   - Scrapple (PA Dutch specialty)
   - Smith Island Cake (Maryland state dessert)
   - Old Bay / J.O. Spice (Baltimore-area seafood seasonings)
   - Beach Plums (Jersey Shore/Delaware coast)
   - Virginia Peanuts (extra-large variety specific to VA)
