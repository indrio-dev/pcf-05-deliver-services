## REGIONAL NOTES

### Harvest Seasons

**Colorado Palisade Peaches:** Late July - Late September (peak August)
**Montana Flathead Cherries:** Mid-July - Mid-August
**Colorado CSA Season:** Generally June - October (17-22 weeks typical)
**Utah Lavender:** End of June - First half of July

### Shipping Regulations

**Wine Shipping:** Colorado law requires out-of-state wineries to obtain Winery Direct Shipper's Permit. For in-state wineries shipping nationally, state-by-state regulations apply.

**Wyoming Food Freedom:** Most permissive in US, allows up to $250K/year in DTC sales, but ONLY intrastate (within Wyoming). Interstate shipping requires federal licenses.

**USDA Processing:** Bison and beef shipped nationally must be processed at USDA-certified facilities.

### Market Trends

**Montana Local Food Sales:** $158M total ($40M DTC, $118M retail/restaurant/institutional)
**Wyoming DTC Growth:** 35% increase in DTC sales 2017-2022
**Utah Food Security Grant:** $1M+ state funding 2022-2025 for processing capacity
**Colorado Honey Production:** Montana typically ranks top 5 states for honey production
