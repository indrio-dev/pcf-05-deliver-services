## SHIPPING NOTES

**Cannot Ship To:**
- **California:** Many farms (especially tropical fruit, avocados, citrus) cannot ship to CA due to agricultural regulations
- **Arizona, Hawaii, Alaska:** Frequently restricted destinations

**Common Shipping Features:**
- Free shipping thresholds typically $50-$150
- Many farms ship via USPS Priority Mail or UPS Ground
- Fresh fruit often ships 1-3 day delivery
- Live animals (fish) ship with heat/cold packs and arrival guarantees
- Eggs may have breakage disclaimers

**Seasonal Availability:**
- Satsumas: November-December
- Strawberries: December-March/April
- Mangoes: May-September
- Lychee/Longan: May-July
- Citrus: November-April
- Many farms year-round with crop rotation
