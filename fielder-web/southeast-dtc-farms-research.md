## REGIONAL NOTES

### Georgia Agriculture
- #1 in muscadine grape production (1,100 acres)
- #1 in peanut production
- Vidalia onions: 10,000 acres planted (2025 season)
- Season: mid-April through early September

### South Carolina Agriculture
- #2 in nation for peach production (outranks Georgia)
- Collard greens: Official state vegetable (since 2011)
- Ships Southern cooking greens to 30 states
- 4 mariculture farms: Lady Island Oyster, Lowcountry Oyster, Barrier Island Oyster, May River Oyster

- #1 in sweet potato production (official state vegetable since 1995)
- #7 in apple production (40+ varieties)
- Fraser fir: 90%+ of NC Christmas trees
- Most popular Christmas tree in North America
- Chosen for White House 16 times (record)
- NC Fraser firs shipped to every US state, Caribbean, Mexico, Canada, Bermuda, Japan
