## NOTES & KEY FINDINGS

### Product Categories Found:
- Apples, pears, cherries (multiple varieties)
- Berries (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, blackberries, marionberries, tayberries, loganberries)
- Hazelnuts (raw, roasted, chocolate-covered)
- Lavender (essential oils, products)
- Tulip bulbs
- Walla Walla sweet onions
- Hops (brewing)
- Wine (Pinot Noir, varietals)
- Grass-fed beef, lamb, pork, chicken
- Rainbow trout, salmon, seafood
- Cheese (sheep, goat, cow)
- Honey
- Mushrooms (gourmet, medicinal)
- Hard cider
- Coffee
- Jams and preserves

### Shipping Models:
1. **Direct Ship Nationwide** - Many specialty producers ship via USPS, UPS, FedEx
2. **CSA with Local Delivery** - Weekly boxes to pickup points or home delivery (regional)
3. **Seasonal Windows** - Tulips (ship Oct-Dec), onions (June-Aug), berries (summer)
4. **Farm Stores + Online** - On-farm retail with e-commerce option
5. **Farmers Markets + Online** - Regular market presence plus web orders

### Geographic Strengths:
- **Yakima Valley**: Dominates US apple production, 75% of US hops
- **Wenatchee Valley**: #1 in state for pears, #2 for cherries, #4 for apples
- **Willamette Valley**: Nearly all US hazelnut production, premium Pinot Noir
- **Hood River**: Nation's largest pear exporter
- **Sequim**: Lavender capital of USA
- **Snake River (ID)**: 70% of US trout farming
- **Skagit Valley**: Largest US tulip/daffodil grower

### Certifications Commonly Seen:
- USDA Organic
- Oregon Tilth Certified Organic
- WSDA Certified Organic
- Animal Welfare Approved
- GAP4 Certified
- NON-GMO Verified
